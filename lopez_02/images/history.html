Ancient Times

In ancient Phoenicia, purple dye was extracted from sea snails and used only by the rich and powerful.

Royal Symbolism

Throughout history, purple has been associated with royalty, spirituality, and wealth, especially in European monarchies.

Purple in Modern Fashion

Today, purple is seen in luxury fashion, makeup, and even pop culture, showing its continued significance.

Purple and Psychology

Purple often symbolizes imagination, peace, and ambition in color psychology.
