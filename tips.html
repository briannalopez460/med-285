Travel Smarter: Must-Know Tips for Your Next Vacation

Packing Tips

  • Use packing cubes to organize and save space.
  • Bring a lightweight scarf — use as a blanket or cover-up.
  • Neutral outfits = more mix and match options.

Tech & Safety

  • Download offline maps for your destination.
  • Enable device tracking in case your phone gets lost.
  • Keep a portable charger handy for long days.

Health & Wellness

  • Use hydration tablets on long flights.
  • Wear sunscreen every day — yes, even when it’s cloudy.
  • Bring a reusable water bottle to stay hydrated.

Budgeting & Money

  • Use a no-foreign-fee travel card.
  • Tell your bank you're traveling.
  • Split up your cash in different bags just in case.
